Get Started

Why should you use LabelAPI?

LabelAPI takes the heavy lifting out of the data so that you can focus on creating great apps. Our existing methods serve a high degree of sophisticated functionality and you can test and play with every one of them on our Interactive Documentation page to see how they work without a lick of code. We even have downloadable starter apps so you can hit the ground running.

How does Free sound?

Zero dollars gets you enough calls to get your app built and tested. Once you launch there is an unlimited use administration fee of only $99 a month. Enterprise levels are at reasonable flat-rates.
