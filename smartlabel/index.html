SmartLabel (™) Transparency Initiative

More Information for More Transparency

This demand for transparency has created the need for a standard way for brands to share product information in a way that consumers can easily access and understand. The launch of the SmartLabel transparency initiative, led by the Grocery Manufacturers Association and Food Marketing Institute, meets this need giving consumers easy, instant access to detailed product information.

Through online landing pages that can be accessed via smartphones, tablets and desktops, customers can access product information organized in a consistent fashion across products. Consumers will be able access detailed product information such as allergens, ingredient sourcing practices, third-party certifications, social compliance and sustainability programs, usage instructions, advisories & safe handling instructions as well as company and brand information.

Going beyond the product label makes sharing additional product information with consumers more attainable and sustainable for brands and retailers. The result is more information, and consequently, more transparency.
